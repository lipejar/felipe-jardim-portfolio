About me

I've always preferred finding solutions over accepting limitations. My curiosity about technology has driven me to explore various areas of IT, from web development to tech support.
Along the way, I worked as a freelance web designer, creating websites that are both functional and visually appealing, and I also independently developed projects in Python.

My projects

Cantinho Lusitano

The Cantinho Lusitano Project is an initiative aimed at bringing the traditional elegance of Lisbon into the online world. Inspired by Lisbon's cultural richness, our goal is to create a website that reflects the authenticity and charm of the Lusitanian spirit. We focus on capturing the classic and captivating essence of the restaurant, offering visitors a virtual experience that transports them to its unique atmosphere.

Michael Jordan Masterclass

This is a landing page project for the Michael Jordan Masterclass. Created with passion and dedication, its goal is to share Jordan's story and his impact on the world of sports. While it’s a fictional and non-profit initiative, the high-conversion page aims to capture the essence of determination and success that define Jordan’s legendary career.

My Maker

MyMaker is an innovative project aimed at the public school student community. Our goal is to inspire, educate, and empower young talents through practical and educational activities. I sought to capture the essence of the project on this page through thoughtful design.

Supertrack

This is a sales page project for the vehicle tracking company Supertrack. Created with passion and dedication, its goal, from the very first to the very last pixel, is to drive conversions. This high-conversion page aims to capture the essence and identity that define Supertrack.
